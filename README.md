# 🚀 Featured Product Case Study

## Recruit-AI — AI Resume Analyzer

A product experiment designed to improve hiring efficiency by augmenting recruiter workflows with AI-assisted candidate evaluation.

Repository  
https://github.com/Veer-Kapoor/Recruit-AI

---

## Product Problem

Recruiters spend significant time manually screening resumes while traditional ATS systems rely heavily on **keyword matching**.

This leads to:

• missed high-potential candidates  
• slow hiring cycles  
• recruiter burnout due to manual screening  

---

## Product Hypothesis

If resume screening incorporates **semantic analysis and structured evaluation**, recruiters can identify stronger candidates faster.

---

## Product Solution

Recruit-AI introduces an **AI-assisted candidate evaluation workflow** that:

• analyzes resumes against job descriptions  
• generates structured candidate scorecards  
• explains reasoning behind candidate fit
